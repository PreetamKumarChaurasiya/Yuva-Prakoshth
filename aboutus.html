About Our Organisation

The All World Gayatri Pariwar (AWGP) stands as a significant global entity, comprising a vast network of millions of volunteers. With over 3000 centers across India and an additional 100 international centers, AWGP orchestrates a myriad of initiatives that aim to propel humanity towards holistic well-being, women's empowerment, societal awakening, thought transformation, and the advent of a new era. The organizational structure of AWGP is intricately divided into zones and subzones. Subzone offices play a pivotal role in maintaining district centers and facilitating coordination with the organizational body. At the district level, centers efficiently organize and manage Mandals, Shaktipeeths, and a dedicated force of volunteers. Mandals, representing small communities of volunteers unified around specific activities, are the bedrock of AWGP's operational structure. These Mandals function closely in conjunction with Shaktipeeths, which serve as administrative hubs and centers for coordination. At a higher echelon, the Chetana Kendras operate as the headquarters, overseeing Subzone/State-level operations with an independent management structure. Shantikunj serves as the headquarters of AWGP, orchestrating and managing all activities and organizational structures. Rooted in the visionary dream of Poojy Gurudev, Pandit Shriram Sharma Acharya, Shantikunj propels new plans, activities, and initiatives. These initiatives aim to facilitate the illuminated ascent of men and women to a higher level of consciousness, ultimately leading to the transformation of society and the alteration of time's trends, paving the way for a new and improved world. This, in essence, is the profound goal of the New Era. The essence of AWGP's existence and the force driving its initiatives is deeply embedded in its volunteers' dedication and the meticulously structured organizational framework. The organization's comprehensive approach and far-reaching initiatives represent a collective endeavor to create a world that thrives on enlightened consciousness, social equity, and progress. AWGP's initiatives in holistic health, women's empowerment, societal awakening, and thought transformation are not just stand-alone efforts; they are threads woven intricately into a larger tapestry that aims to elevate human consciousness and bring about a transformative shift in society. Shantikunj and AWGP's commitment to the dream envisioned by Pandit Shriram Sharma Acharya serves as the guiding light, motivating volunteers to work tirelessly and passionately toward the realization of a future where humanity ascends to new heights of consciousness, fostering a world that embraces harmony, evolution, and enlightenment. This collective effort resonates with the vision of a world where men and women are enlightened, leading to the inception of a new era—a vision AWGP strives ardently to fulfill.

AGayatri Pariwar is a living model of a futuristic society, being guided by the principles of human unity and equality. It's a modern adaptation of the age-old wisdom of Vedic Rishis, who practiced and propagated the philosophy of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam, which literally means “the whole world is one family”. Founded by saint, reformer, writer, philosopher, spiritual guide and visionary Yug Rishi Vedmurti Taponishth Pandit Shriram Sharma Acharya Ji, this mission has emerged as a mass movement for the great Transformation of the Era. Aims & Objectives: • Rise of divinity in humankind, descent of heaven on earth • Individual Development, Family Development and Social Upliftment • Healthy body, pure mind and civilized society • “Atmavat sarvabhooteshu” (all living beings are soulkins), Vasudhaiv kutumbkam (Entire earth is one family) • One Nation, One Language (love), One Religion (humanity), One Government (Self Govern) • Everyone should get equal opportunity for self-growth irrespective of caste, color or creed

Vision

Mission

Our mission is the creation of such a society where nobody is deprived of the main current of development. We are determined for the establishment of that society in which everybody possesses ‘Human rights’ with dignity.
